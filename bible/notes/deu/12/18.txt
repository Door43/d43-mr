===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.
  * **you will eat them** - "you will eat your offerings"
  * **before Yahweh** - "in the presence of Yahweh"
  * **within your gate** - AT: “inside your city” (See: [[:mr:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **everything that you put your hand to** - AT: "all you undertake" or "all that you do" (See: [[:mr:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Pay attention to yourself ** - "Be careful"
  * **that you do not forsake** - Moses uses a negative comment to emphasize that the people must take care of the Levites. (See: [[:mr:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
