===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **you will sleep with your fathers**  - AT: "you will die and join your ancestors who have died before you" (See: [[:mr:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
  * **this people will rise up**  - AT: "this people of Israel will take new action"
  * **act like prostitutes**  - Just as a prostitute has sex with many strangers, so the Israelites will submit themselves to gods other than their one true God, Yahweh. (See: [[:mr:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **break my covenant that I have made with them**  - disobey the covenant that I, Yahweh, have made with them. (See: [[:mr:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
