===== Translation Notes: =====

Asaph is pleading for God's help.

  * **Call to mind**  - AT: "Remember" 
  * **the soul of your dove**  - The phrase "your dove" refers to the people of Israel. (See: [[:mr:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **a wild animal**  - This phrase compares the enemies of Israel to "a wild animal." AT: "a savage enemy that is like a wild animal." (See: [[mr:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
