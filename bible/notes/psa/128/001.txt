===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[:mr:ta:workbench:translate:poetry|Translating Poetry]] and [[:mr:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]]

  * **Song of Ascents**- See how you translated this in [[:mr:bible:notes:psa:120:001|120:1]]
  * **Blessed is everyone who honors Yahweh** - "Yahweh will bless everyone who honors him" (See: [[:mr:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **What your hands provide**  - Here a man is being referred to by his hands because that is the part of the body he works with. AT: "What you provide" or "What you work for" (See: [[mr:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **blessed and prosper**  - These two words emphasize God's favor. (See: [[mr:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
