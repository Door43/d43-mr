===== Translation Notes: =====

This Psalm begins with a group of people speaking to the king of Israel.
See: [[mr:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:mr:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]

  * **musician** - The word "musician" refers to one who writes, sings, or plays music.
  * **A psalm of David** - This can also be written as a sentence. AT: "This is a psalm of David" or "This is a psalm that David wrote"
  * **help you** - The word "you" in this Psalm is singular.
  * **the name of the God** - AT: "the God" (See: [[:mr:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
