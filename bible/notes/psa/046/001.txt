===== Translation Notes: =====
See: [[mr:ta:workbench:translate:poetry]] and [[:mr:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] 

This psalm is a song of praise.

  * **Alamoth** - This is a musical term, possibly the name of a medley. (See: [[:mr:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
  * **God is our refuge and strength** - God is described as a strong place, safe from attack. AT: "God is the reason we are safe" (See: [[:mr:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the heart of the sea**- The sea depths are inside the sea like a heart is inside of a person or animal. AT: "into the depths of the sea" (See: [[:mr:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **mountains tremble with their swelling** - This phrase describes a volcano that is about to erupt. This is an event that would cause fear if a person did not have confidence they were protected by God. AT: "though the mountains tremble as they swell" or "though the volcanoes tremble as they swell"
