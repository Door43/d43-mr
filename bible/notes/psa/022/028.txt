===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **For the kingdom is Yahweh’s** - "For the kingdom belongs to Yahweh" AT: "For Yahweh is king" (UDB)
  * **those who are descending into the dust** - AT: "those who are dying" (See: [[:mr:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]] and [[:mr:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **those who cannot preserve their own souls** - AT: "those who cannot keep their own souls alive" or "those who cannot save themselves" (See: [[:mr:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
