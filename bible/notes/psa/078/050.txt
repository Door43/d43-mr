===== Translation Notes: =====

The writer continues to describe what God did to the Egyptians.

  * **He leveled a path for his anger**  - AT: "He was so angry that he did everything he could to harm them" or "It was as though his anger was an army and he made a smooth road for it to march on." (See: [[mr:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
  * **spare them from death** - "keep them from dying" or "permit them to live"
  * **the firstborn of their strength** - "their best and most important males"
  * **in the tents of Ham** - "among the families of Egypt" (See: [[mr:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
