===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They are acting now the same as they did since the day I brought them out of Egypt**  - Yahweh is reminding Samuel of the way Israel acted many years ago when his servant Moses led the Israelites out of Egypt where they were slaves for 400 years. (See: [[:mr:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])  
  * **but warn them solemnly**  - "but tell them very seriously"
  * **now listen to them;**  - "now do what they ask you to do;"
