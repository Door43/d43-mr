===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **my son David**  - Saul established his desired (not actual) relationship with David by using the term "son." (See: [[:mr:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]],  [[:mr:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
    * **What have I done? What evil is in my hand?**  - This repetition of a question becomes a doublet, emphasizing the point made. See: [[:mr:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]]
