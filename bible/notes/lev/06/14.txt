====== Leviticus 06:14-15 ======

===== TFT: =====

<usfm>
\v 14 "These are the regulations concerning the offerings made from grain: Aaron's sons must bring them to Yahweh in front of the altar.
\v 15 The priest must take a handful of fine flour mixed with olive oil and incense and burn that on the altar. That handful will signify that the whole offering truly belongs to Yahweh. And the aroma while it burns will be pleasing to Yahweh.
