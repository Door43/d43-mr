===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh tells Moses and Aaron what the people must do when someone is cleansed of a skin disease.

  * **He must be brought to the priest **- AT: "Someone must bring him to the priest" or "He must go to the priest" (See: [[mr:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
