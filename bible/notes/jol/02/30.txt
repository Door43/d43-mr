===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues speaking of things he will do in the future.

  * **the heavens and on the earth** - This is a phrase that refers to the whole creation. 
  * **blood, fire and pillars of smoke** - "blood" is symbolizing the death of people. AT: "death, fire and pillars of smoke" (See: [[:mr:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **and the moon into blood** - AT: "and the moon will turn red" (See: [[:mr:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **great and terrible day** - AT: "an important, fearful day" (See: [[:mr:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
