===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You must give** - Here the word "You" refers to Moses.
  * **They are wholly given** - AT: "I have given them entirely" (See: [[:mr:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **but any foreigner who comes near** - AT: "but any foreigner who comes near the tabernacle" (See: [[:mr:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **must be put to death** - AT: "you must kill" or "must die" (See: [[:mr:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
