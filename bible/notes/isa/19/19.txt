===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It will be as a sign and a witness to Yahweh of Hosts** – The full meaning of "sign" and "witness" can be made explicit: “The alter and the pillar will show that the people of Egypt worship Yahweh of Hosts.” (See: [[:mr:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Yahweh of Hosts** - See how you translated this in [[mr:bible:notes:isa:01:09|1:9]].
  * **When they cry** - "When the Egyptians cry"
  * **because of oppressors** – "because someone is treating them harshly" or "because others are causing them to suffer"
  * **he will send them a savior and a defender** - "he will send someone to save and defend them"
